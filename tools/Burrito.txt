The Burrito system is a Linux-based provenance collection and note-taking tool that runs in the background of the operating system. While in the background, Burrito collects system-level provenance from every computational process running in the operating system. In addition, it captures information regarding GUI window interactions and uses a versioning file system to preserve a complete history of the system.
