Noweb

Site: http://www.cs.tufts.edu/~nr/noweb/

Source: ftp://www.eecs.harvard.edu/pub/nr

Noweb supports a variety of text formats, such as TeX, LaTeX and HTML. However, there is no prettyprinting involved, i.e., no stylistic formatting supported (stylized fonts, mathematical symbols and indentations).
